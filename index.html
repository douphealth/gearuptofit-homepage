Running Shoe Reviews, Fitness Calculators & Training Tools for Smarter Fitness

GearUpToFit is an independent fitness publication and tools hub helping runners and active people choose better running shoes, compare fitness watches and smartwatches, match evidence-based supplements, build a free 8-week fitness plan, and use free science-based calculators for BMI, TDEE, macros, heart-rate zones, VO2 max, and running pace.

Free Fitness Tools

  • Running Shoe Finder — Personalized running shoe recommendations based on foot type, weekly mileage, terrain, cushioning, support needs, and budget.
  • Watch Match — Match the right fitness watch, running watch, or smartwatch by GPS accuracy, battery life, heart-rate tracking, training metrics, and price.
  • Supplement Match — Evidence-based vitamin and supplement quiz tailored to training goals, diet, and health profile.
  • Body Recomp OS — Free 8-week fitness planner for strength, fat loss, and body recomposition.
  • Fitness & Health Calculators — 16+ calculators: BMI, TDEE, macros, body fat, calorie burn, heart-rate zones, running pace, one-rep max, VO2 max, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is GearUpToFit?

GearUpToFit is a running gear, fitness tools, fitness watch, training plan, and calculator website that helps users choose running shoes, compare fitness watches, use calculators, and build smarter fitness plans.

Are GearUpToFit's tools free?

Yes. The Running Shoe Finder, Watch Match, Supplement Match, Fitness Plan, and all 16+ fitness and health calculators are free to use, with no signup required.

How does GearUpToFit make recommendations?

Recommendations are evidence-aware and based on published sports-science research, real-world testing, and transparent quiz logic. Some pages include clearly disclosed affiliate links, but recommendations stay user-first.

Is GearUpToFit medical advice?

No. GearUpToFit provides educational fitness information and planning tools. Consult a qualified professional for medical, injury, or health-specific advice.
